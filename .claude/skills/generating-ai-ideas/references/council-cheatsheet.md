# Idea Generator — Council Cheat Sheet

**Official Modes (from 20-run experiments)**

## Fast Mode (Core 3)
**Use for**: Quick filtering, early exploration, high-volume screening.

**Team**: Moonshot + Antagonizer + Researcher

**Strengths**: Highest signal-to-noise, fast, strong idea generation.
**Limitations**: Can miss deep operational, emotional, or distribution realities.

**Log as**: `Fast Mode (Core 3)`

---

## Standard Strong Mode (Recommended Default)
**Use for**: Most serious council work.

**Core (always)**: Antagonizer + Researcher

**Add selectively**:

| Add This Role                        | When the Seed Involves...                              | Typical Pairing |
|--------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------|-----------------|
| **User Reality**                     | Emotion, identity, stigma, burnout, family dynamics, creators, educators, mental health | Highest ROI on human-heavy topics |
| **Differentiation & Moat**           | Need strong ownability / competitive edge (most cases) | Best with Antagonizer |
| **Distribution & Channel Realist**   | Marketplace, platform, "how do we actually reach them?" | Marketplace & acquisition ideas |
| **COO**                              | Heavy maintenance burden, operations, support load     | Agent/SaaS |
| **Legal**                            | Liability, privacy, regulated, hardware                | Hardware + high-risk |

**Target size**: 4–5 people (max 6)

**Log as**: `Standard Strong Mode: Antagonizer + Researcher + User Reality + Differentiation`

---

## Quick Decision Guide

**Start here**:
1. Always begin with **Antagonizer + Researcher**.
2. Add **User Reality** if the product touches humans emotionally or their daily life.
3. Add **Differentiation & Moat** in most cases.
4. Add **Distribution** if it's a marketplace or platform play.
5. Add **COO / Legal** only when operations or risk are central.
6. Stop when additional voices stop meaningfully changing the output.

**Common Winning Teams** (from 20 experiments):

- **Emotional / Creator / Educator / Hardware**: Antagonizer + Researcher + User Reality + Differentiation
- **Marketplace / Platform**: Antagonizer + Researcher + Differentiation + Distribution (+ COO)
- **Agent/SaaS with maintenance burden**: Kill Team 4 = Antagonizer + Researcher + COO + Legal
- **Fast triage**: Core 3 only

**Rule (updated from live runs)**: 
- For broad requests, start in Fast Mode (Core 3) for triage before committing to Standard Strong.
- Smaller, high-agency teams (4-5) beat large balanced councils on quality and conviction.
